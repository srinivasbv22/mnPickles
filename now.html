Inception and Now

Way back in 1982, our founder Shri Masur Narayanappa started it right from home with humble 200 kilos of pickles having a great vision to reach people of India with mouth watering, lip smacking pickles. Now reaching it's best and growing further our annual production has crossed 1200 tons.

Situated at Siralakoppa, Shikaripur taluk of Shimoga district in Karnataka, we manufacture all the products hygienically using advanced machinery and technology. We stand by our motto, procuring raw materials, locally grown fruits and vegetables from local farmers, employing local people, and manufacturing quality products.

We've outsourced export of our products to USA, Canada, Britain, Malaysia, Singapore, Austria, UAE, New Zealand and various other countries.
